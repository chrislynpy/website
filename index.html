A bit about me

I'm Chrislyn — I love figuring things out, whether that's why a campaign flopped, why my chillies won't fruit, or why that new tool everyone's talking about actually works.

By day I work in marketing and consumer insights — the kind of work where you spend a lot of time asking "but why did they actually do that?" and then building models to find out. I genuinely love it. Numbers that tell human stories are endlessly fascinating to me.

By weekend I'm usually in the garden, covered in soil, probably listening to a podcast, definitely neglecting my inbox. I grow herbs, vegetables, and the occasional thing that has no business surviving a Singapore balcony. It keeps me humble.

I started writing here because I wanted one place where the gardening and the strategy and the tech obsessions could coexist without apology. If any of it resonates — or if you just want to talk about why your lemongrass isn't thriving — I'd love to hear from you.
